About

Who We Are

Our Vision: To become a global leader in future mobility systems and simulation-driven transportation technologies.

Apex Motion is a future mobility systems startup that builds next-gen vehicle platforms through a fusion of smart hardware, digital twins, and immersive interfaces. From real-time control to AR/VR maintenance and virtual simulation, we integrate intelligence into every moving part. Our team of engineers, designers, and innovators is focused on building virtual-first platforms for electric and autonomous vehicles.

Core Values:

  • Innovation
  • Precision
  • Sustainability
  • Immersive Engineering

We specialize in blending real-time data, predictive intelligence, and immersive interfaces to enhance vehicle reliability, fleet performance, and user experience.

What We’ve Built

  • Predictive Maintenance Prototype for 2W/3W Electric Platforms

  • AR-Guided Repair Interface for Offsite Service Teams

EV Health Management System for Urban Fleet Operators

A robust, cloud-integrated platform that monitors the vital parameters of electric vehicles in real-time, including battery health, motor efficiency, thermal patterns, and trip analytics. Designed for last-mile delivery and ride-sharing fleets, this system ensures operational transparency, preventive alerts, and extended vehicle life cycles.

Predictive Maintenance Prototype for 2W/3W Electric Platforms

A lightweight, edge-computing-based prototype tailored for two- and three-wheeler EVs. It continuously assesses component wear-and-tear using historical usage data and live performance metrics, offering intelligent maintenance alerts to minimize service downtimes and maximize fleet availability.

AR-Guided Repair Interface for Offsite Service Teams

An augmented reality application that overlays diagnostic information, repair instructions, and component visuals directly on physical vehicle systems. Optimized for use by remote service technicians, this solution reduces dependency on manuals and enhances first-time fix rates.

Digital Twin Pilot for Tier-1 Automotive OEM

UA pilot project deployed with a Tier-1 automotive supplier, creating real-time virtual replicas of vehicle subsystems for testing, validation, and condition monitoring. This platform serves as the foundation for model-based design, control validation, and remote troubleshooting workflows.
